Services

What we build and maintain

Focused packages for owner-operated businesses that need a credible online footprint.

Online Presence Setup

Google profile setup plus a clean one-page website for core business information.

  • Google Business Profile setup/cleanup
  • One-page website (Squarespace or equivalent)
  • Basic on-page SEO and tracking handoff

Monthly Presence Management

Ongoing upkeep so your listings and site stay accurate and active.

  • Profile and listing updates
  • Posting cadence for visibility (as appropriate)
  • Light website edits and content refresh

Social Media Setup

Clean profiles and a practical baseline that matches your business identity.

  • Profile creation or refresh
  • Brand-consistent bio, photos, links
  • Starter content structure (what to post, when)

Review System Setup

Simple, compliant review flow that’s easy for customers to use.

  • QR code + review link setup
  • On-site signage guidance (print-ready file)
  • Staff-friendly instructions for asking reviews
Process

A straightforward workflow

Four steps that keep scope clear and timelines predictable.

Audit

We review your current presence (search results, listings, site, and social) and identify the highest-impact fixes.

Setup

We implement the agreed scope: profiles, website, review flow, and access structure under your ownership.

Launch

We publish and verify key items, confirm the customer path (search → trust → contact), and document what’s live.

Maintain

Monthly updates keep information accurate, support review momentum, and prevent the usual drift over time.

FAQs

Common questions

Clear answers so you can decide quickly.

Do you require long-term contracts?
No. Work is scoped and quoted per business. Monthly management can be started or paused as needed.
How long does setup take?
Typically 5–7 days once access and required information are provided.
Do you build on Squarespace?
Yes. Sites are built in a client-owned Squarespace account (or transferred to one you own).
Do you manage reviews?
Yes, as part of management. We support review requests, monitor new reviews, and help draft appropriate responses.
